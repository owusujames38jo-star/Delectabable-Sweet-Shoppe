Our Story: Baked with Passion

Divine Delights was founded in 2018 by Master Baker Sheree Thomas, who turned a lifelong passion for fermentation and flaky pastry into a thriving neighborhood business. We believe that the best food is made by hand, with simple, quality ingredients, and a generous amount of time.

Our Commitment to Quality

Our philosophy is simple: **Source the best, bake the best.** We use locally milled organic flour, European-style butter, and natural yeast cultures. We never rush our process. Our sourdough is fermented for 24 hours, and our croissants proof overnight to achieve that perfect, airy honeycomb interior.
