⚠️ Ingredients & Allergen Information

The health and safety of our customers is our top priority. Please review this information carefully before placing an order.

Major Allergens Disclaimer

WARNING: All products are made in a kitchen that handles **wheat, milk, eggs, tree nuts (almonds, walnuts, pecans), peanuts, and soy**. We cannot guarantee that any item is free of trace amounts of these allergens due to shared equipment.

Ingredients Philosophy

We commit to using high-quality ingredients, including organic flours, European butter, and natural flavorings. No artificial preservatives are used in our fresh products.

  • Flour: Locally sourced, organic, unbleached wheat flour.
  • Dairy: High-quality European-style butter and whole milk.
  • Leavening: Natural sourdough cultures or non-aluminum baking powder/soda.

Specific Allergies

If you have a severe food allergy, please contact us directly at order@divinedelights.com BEFORE ordering. We are happy to discuss specific ingredient lists for individual products.

About Delectable Sweet Shoppe

We bake with a commitment to quality, using only the finest ingredients sourced locally whenever possible. Every loaf and pastry is a labor of love.
