We offer flexible options for getting our fresh baked goods into your hands, whether you prefer local delivery or a quick pickup at the shop!

In-Store Pickup

Place your order online and pick it up at our Artisan Lane location. This is the fastest and easiest way to grab your items!

  • Hours: Available during our operating hours (Mon-Sat).
  • Timing: Orders are typically ready 30 minutes after placement (or at your specified time).
  • Location: 123 Artisan Lane, Bakeville, CA 90210.

Local Delivery

We deliver within a **10-mile radius** of the bakery. Delivery is managed by our in-house team to ensure freshness.

  • Fees: $5 flat fee for orders under $50. Free delivery for orders over $50.
  • Schedule: Deliveries are made Tuesday - Friday between 10:00 AM and 3:00 PM.
  • Process: You will receive a notification 15 minutes before arrival.

Shipping (Perishables)

Currently, we do not offer nationwide shipping for highly perishable goods like fresh pastries or cakes. Only our sealed coffee and merchandise can be shipped.

About Delectable Sweet Shoppe

We bake with a commitment to quality, using only the finest ingredients sourced locally whenever possible. Every loaf and pastry is a labor of love.
