Find quick answers to our most common questions regarding orders, freshness, and our baking process.

How long will my bread stay fresh?

Our artisan loaves are best enjoyed within **3 days** of purchase. To maximize freshness, store them at room temperature in a paper bag (not plastic) or freeze them for up to one month.

Can I place a custom cake order?

Yes! We specialize in custom wedding and celebration cakes. Please visit our Contact page and fill out the inquiry form at least **two weeks** in advance of your event date.

Are there any gluten-free options?

While we do offer a few items made with non-wheat flour (like our Almond Coconut Macaroons), we are NOT a certified gluten-free kitchen. Please see our Allergens page for a full disclaimer.
